MISSION

Sunrise School’s reason for being is rooted in enriching the community by providing high-quality educational services.

Through the transformative power of education and exposure to new ideas and ways of thinking, we aim to develop skills and talents, while inspiring the achievement of goals in a safe environment — all in order to better serve a global society.

VISION

To be an internationally renowned educational institution, focused on human development within an innovative, open, and participatory framework.

By providing creative, holistic, and intellectual training, we ensure student success.

VALUES

Respect, responsibility, discipline, empathy, courage, and collaboration.

Sunrise’s values are rooted in preserving the unity between school and home, providing our students with a healthy, inclusive environment that supports their well-being and academic success.
